The Best Movies According to Ian

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption

As a title suggests, a film about redemption where a man who is falsely imprisoned makes the best of a bad situation while plotting to escape.

Pulp Fiction

Makes good use of nonlinear story-telling and ties together multiple character's stories well. Requires a couple of watches to understand the timeline.

Once Upon a Time in Hollywood

The setting/world that they built paired with memorable performances from Brad Pitt and Leonardo DiCaprio make this easy to watch again and again.
